Short Bio

My name is Dylan Cooper, and I grew up in Staten Island and Pennsylvania.
After graduating high school, I joined the Army to become an Air Traffic Controller.
I served in the Army for 8 years before transitioning to the same role with the FAA.

During my time with the FAA, I worked in three different locations: Oklahoma City, Memphis, and Alaska.
All of these assignments taught me valuable skills in communication, teamwork, and critical thinking.
While I enjoyed the work, I've always had a passion for software development and coding.

That's why I'm now pursuing a career in software development.
I'm currently taking courses and working on personal projects to build my skills and experience.
My goal is to become a skilled software developer and contribute to meaningful projects.
